Understanding Bed Bugs

Bed bugs are tiny, brownish insects that are able to hide in tight spaces such as mattresses seams and bed frames as well as furniture joint. They generally emerge in the evening to eat. If you notice tiny bite marks that are red across your face, a few dark marks on your mattress or a musty odor it is likely that you require bed bug spray immediately.
