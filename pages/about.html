Our Story

M/s Al Sami Associates (Private) Limited was established in January 2014 as a private construction and maintenance firm. The objective of its creation is to create a pool of talented professionals dedicated to delivering excellence in construction, renovation, and allied services.

Over the years, we have grown from a small construction company to a recognized name in the industry, serving government institutions, private organizations, and individual clients across Pakistan. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and client satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

We specialize in complete construction projects from initial planning and mapping to finished buildings, including extensions, renovations, and maintenance services. Our team of experienced engineers and contractors ensures that every project meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

As a registered company with Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC), Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MoFA), and AGPR, we maintain the highest standards of professionalism and compliance in all our operations.

Our Journey

2014 - Foundation

Al Sami Associates (Private) Limited was established in January 2014, marking the beginning of our journey in construction and maintenance services.
