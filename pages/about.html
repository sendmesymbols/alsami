Who We Are

M/s Al Sami Associates (Private) Limited, established in January 2014, is known as a private construction & maintenance firm. The objective of its creation is to create a pool of talented company by engineering practice, attain self-reliance in engineering construction, and replace the non-technical, local practice in construction projects in Pakistan.
