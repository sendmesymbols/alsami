About Joint Sealant

JOINT SEALANT is one part self-leveling, moisture curing, rubberized joint sealant specifically developed to form a permanent watertight seal.

This advanced formula is designed to handle the expansion and contraction of building joints while maintaining its integrity. It is ideal for horizontal joints in concrete structures where a durable, flexible seal is required to prevent water ingress.
