About Elastic Seal

Elastic Seal is an advanced waterproofing membrane designed to provide superior protection against water ingress for a variety of structures.

Its highly elastic nature allows it to bridge cracks and accommodate structural movements without compromising its sealing integrity. Ideal for roofs, basements, and wet areas, Elastic Seal offers a long-lasting solution to common waterproofing challenges.
