About Kaufseal KM 42

KAUFSEAL KM 42 is a two-component flexible cementitious coating specifically formulated for heavy-duty waterproofing.

Supplied in a prepackaged form consisting of a liquid polymer component and a cementitious powder, it is mixed on-site to create a brushable or sprayable slurry. Once cured, it forms a tough, flexible membrane that is resistant to water pressure and environmental stressors.
