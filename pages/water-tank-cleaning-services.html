Why Water Tank Cleaning is Important?

In time, dirt, algae and bacteria build up within your tank. This can lead to serious health problems. Regular water tank cleaning services help you:

  • Get rid of harmful algae and harmful bacteria.
  • Improve the water quality and hygiene
  • Eliminate bad odor and contamination
  • Keep tank life in check and maintain structure

We ensure deep cleaning and disinfection of your tanks with proven and safe chemicals.

Our Water Tank Cleaning Process

Our expert team uses a six-step cleaning method:

  1. Drainage: Complete removal of filthy water
  2. Elimination of Sludge: The elimination of all mud as well as leftovers
  3. HPJ Cleaning: The deep cleaning of walls and bases
  4. Anti-Bacterial Treatment: Killing all harmful germs
  5. UV treatment (Optional): For hospitals and patients with sensitive skin
  6. Refilling and final inspection: ensuring crystal clear, secure water
