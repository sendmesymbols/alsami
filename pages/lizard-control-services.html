Why Lizard Control is Important

Lizards are prevalent in Pakistani homes because of the humid and warm conditions. They can be found in cupboards or ceilings and even lights and are disgusting and unhygienic. While the majority of lizards are harmless however, they can infect food as well as spread bacteria and cause anxiety.

Our Lizard Control Process

1. Site Inspection

Our pest control experts will first examine your home to find areas of lizards’ hiding and entry points such as drains, windows, and air vents.

2. Chemical & Non-Chemical Treatment

We utilize eco-friendly pesticides, lizard repellents and repellent sprays to get rid of Lizards and to prevent their re-entry. The products we employ are approved by the WHO and safe for human consumption.

3. Seal Entry Points

We block areas of entry like cracks, pipe holes and ventilation gaps to ensure that there are no lizards that can get into your property.

Preventive Tips to Keep Lizards Away

  • Make sure your bathrooms and kitchens are dry.
  • Shut off windows and doors properly.
  • Window nets are a good way to block the entry.
  • Avoid keeping leftover food uncovered.
  • Clean regularly the ceilings and lights.
