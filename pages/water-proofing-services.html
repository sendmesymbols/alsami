Why Roof Waterproofing Is Important

Roof leakage is among the most prevalent problems found in commercial and residential buildings as well as homes in Pakistan. If there isn’t proper rain-proofing of the roof rainwater may result in:

  • Ceilings that are damp and walls
  • Damage to paint and fungus
  • Structural weakening
  • Water seepage into electrical systems

The roofing waterproofing solutions is designed to fix these issues permanently and prolong the lifespan the roof.
