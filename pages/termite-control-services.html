Why Termite Treatment Is Important

Termites, locally referred to as Deemak they are one of some of the worst pests that live in Pakistan. They thrive in humid, dark conditions and eat the cellulose in paper, wood, as well as drywall. If they are not treated promptly they can cause structural damage that is serious and cost thousands in repairs.

The use of a professional termite control is the most tested solution to eradicate the pests and avoid the possibility of future infestations.

Our Termite Treatment Process

  1. Site Inspection: Our team starts with an in-depth examination of your home to determine the kind and severity of the infestation.
  2. Drilling & Chemical Injection: We make small holes in the areas that are affected — such as around the door frames, walls and floors and inject a high-quality termite spray.
  3. Use of High-Quality Chemicals: We employ chemical products that have been approved by industry like boric acid, fipronil as well as imidacloprid.
  4. Surface & Soil Treatment: Alongside drilling, we also treat wood and soil to stop future attacks by termites.
  5. Sealing and Cleaning: Following the treatment after the treatment, all holes are closed well, and the entire surface is cleaned.
  6. Final Monitoring & Warranty: After the treatment is completed, our specialists examine the treated areas to verify the effectiveness.

Types of Termite Treatments We Offer

1 – Pre-Construction Termite Treatment

Prior to laying the foundation before laying the foundation, we apply chemical treatments to prevent termites on the soil. This will ensure that termites won’t enter your home in the near future.

2 – Post-Construction Termite Treatment

If your structure is built, we can provide termite control after construction through the injection of chemicals in floors, walls as well as wooden structure.

3 – Wood Protection Treatment

We offer termite treatment for wood using the most advanced chemical coatings that shield furniture, doors and other wooden objects from future attacks by termites.

Termite Treatment Chemicals We Use

  • Boric Acid: Natural, safe chemical that effectively eliminates termites through dehydration.
  • Fipronil: An extremely potent chemical which is able to work through ingestion and contact to destroy whole termite colonies.
  • Imidacloprid: A modern chemical utilised for pest control services to provide protection for the long term.
  • Chlorpyrifos: Most often employed to treat soil and for foundation protection.
